Intro

Hi there! My name is Prarup Manandhar, and I am a passionate UI/UX designer with more than 2 years of experience in the industry. I specialize in creating intuitive, user-friendly interfaces that deliver a seamless user experience.
As a designer, I am driven by a user-centered approach and always strive to understand the needs and preferences of my target audience. I am skilled in conducting user research, creating user personas, wireframing, prototyping, and conducting usability testing. I am proficient in using design tools such as Sketch, Adobe XD, Figma, and InVision, and I am always eager to learn and adapt to new design technologies and trends.
I have a proven track record of delivering successful design solutions for various clients, ranging from startups to established brands. I have worked on projects across a wide range of industries, including e-commerce, healthcare, education, and finance. Apart from designing, I enjoy traveling, exploring new cultures, and trying out new cuisines. I am passionate about diversity and inclusivity and believe that it is important to create design solutions that cater to the needs of users from different backgrounds and perspectives.
If you are looking for a designer who can help you create exceptional user experiences, feel free to contact me. I would love to collaborate with you and help bring your ideas to life. Check out my work.

Portfolio

As a UI/UX designer, I have had the opportunity to work on a wide range of design projects, from mobile apps to responsive web designs. My role has involved working collaboratively with clients, developers, and other designers to ensure that the final product is visually appealing, intuitive to use, and aligned with the client's business objectives. To achieve this, I have utilized a variety of design tools, such as Sketch, Adobe XD, and Figma, and have also conducted user research and testing to inform the design decisions. I have also developed a strong understanding of design principles and have used this knowledge to create engaging user experiences that are accessible to all users. Overall, my experience as a UI/UX designer has taught me the importance of balancing aesthetics with functionality to create designs that truly meet the needs of the user.
